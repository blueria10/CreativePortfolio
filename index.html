Tech Projects

When I was a computer science major, my first 2 years of college, I focused a lot on creating personal projects with a focus of using coding languages such as python, Flask, java, etc. Tech projects allowed me to further my skills and apply tech to real life projects. But my goal was to create projects that could help society like my SMS app that helps people identify unknown phone numbers for safety purposes.

Not just that, but I also have a weird fascination with electrical engineering and have completed a lot of courses related to building projects using electronics. Such as the autonomous car I built with some friends using Raspberry Pi. In addition to this project was C.H.I.M.E, a Cardiac Heart Infant Murmur Educator which is a sensor-based training device designed to help healthcare professionals have a real-world experience of listening and identifying heart murmurs.

Exhibitions

// EXH_ARCHIVE_2026

a bug's life Rice University

A life-size puppet theater made of trash. I wanted to force empathy for the small things we usually ignore. It was about sustainability and recontextualization.

  • HARDWARE: High-Res Projector, Stereo Speakers, HDMI
  • SOFTWARE: Adobe Premiere Pro (AV Sync)
  • MATERIALS: Reclaimed Wood, Nails, Tape, Wires
the flocking Stairwell B

A site-specific experiment and exhibition on human "sheep" movement. I used a projector on a chair to represent a moment of "rest" in a high-traffic stairwell.

  • HARDWARE: Digital Projector, Pedestal Chair
  • CONCEPTS: Social Dynamics, Flocking Behavior
limen + fire Sleepy Cyborg

Projection mapping (Feb 2025). A two-part installation at the intersection of African spirituality and quantum mechanics. I created the projection-mapped layer where shadow figures flee from fire and bombs, transforming a static painting into a path of escape between physical and metaphysical realms. The hardware failed and the some data files are gone, but the installation was real :/.

  • HARDWARE: Digital Projector, Projection Surface (Limen Painting)
  • SOFTWARE: After Effects, Audition, Projection Mapping Tools
  • CONCEPTS: Hybridity, Quantum Mechanics, Shadow Work
