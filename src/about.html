About

My name is Emmanuella Desruisseaux and I am a student currently pursuing a B.A. in Cognitive Science and Computer Science and a minor in Engineering Design at Rice University in Houston, TX. Although, based in Boston, MA! My work spans across various disciplines, from analyzing data to managing complex VR projects to creating immersive multimedia installations. I have a keen interest in sustainable practices, exemplified by my life-size puppet theater project made entirely from recycled and reusable materials. This project allowed me to explore the intersection of technology, art, and sustainability, using advanced projection techniques and sound design to convey powerful narratives. I’m skilled in multiple programming languages and tools, including Python, Java, C#, and Arduino, and have hands-on experience in prototyping, fabrication, and UX/UI design. My technical expertise is complemented by a strong foundation in cognitive science, enabling me to approach problems with a unique, interdisciplinary perspective.

In my free time, I enjoy playing volleyball, running, and exploring new technologies like Unity and the intersection of data and writing, which I’m excited to incorporate into future projects. My goal is to create impactful, meaningful experiences that combine the best of technology, creativity, and society. Current interests: Data Journalism, human-computer interaction, sensor technologies, and learning Spanish! You will find me to be a dedicated and ready to learn professional who is eager to innovate and contribute to impactful projects. Let’s connect and explore how we can create something amazing together!
