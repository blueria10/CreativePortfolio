UX / Interface Design

12twenty redesign

Usability Testing Heuristic Evaluation Prototyping Interaction Design Figma

Rice University students rely on the 12twenty career portal to navigate internships, full-time roles, and employer relationships, yet the platform frequently added confusion to an already stressful process. The fragmented "Employers," "Target Employers," and "Contacts" tabs increased cognitive load at the exact moments students needed clarity.

This redesign merges the three tabs into a unified "Employer Network," reorganizing filters around student mental models, simplifying navigation, and redesigning the employer detail page to surface what actually matters during a job search.

esther redesign

Literature Review Card Sorting Usability Testing Comparative Benchmarking Figma

Esther is Rice University's self-service student portal for registration, financial aid, and academic records. Students, faculty, and alumni frequently encountered issues with navigation, consistency, and accessibility that hindered their ability to access services effectively.

My partner and I conducted a full usability evaluation and redesign, including literature review, comparative benchmarking, card sorting, usability testing, and flow chart documentation, to create a user-centered solution for a complex, high-stakes system.
